How it works

Not black-box AI. A structured behavioural engine.

Aletheai reflects how people actually make decisions — observable behaviour, structured logic, transparent reasoning at every step.

  • S
    Signals Browsing patterns, returns to listings, AIP timing, communication cadence, market shifts — the observable behaviour your CRM already captures but doesn't act on.
  • B
    Behaviour patterns Signals are interpreted as patterns: re-engagement, budget shift, intent decay, readiness escalation. Each pattern has a known meaning, not a probability score.
  • D
    Decision logic Patterns trigger structured decision rules — explainable, auditable, and tunable. The output is a ranked action list with a clear "why" for every entry.
